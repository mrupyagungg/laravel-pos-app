    ## ✨ Laravel Point of Sale

Point of Sale Management and Invoice build with Laravel 10 and MySql.

## Features
- POS
- Orders
  - Pending Orders
  - Complete Orders
  - Pending Due
- Stock Management
- Products
  - Products
  - Categories
- Employees
- Customers
- Suppliers
- Salary
  - Advance Salary
  - Pay Salary
  - History Pay Salary
- Attendance
- Role and Permission
- Users Management
- Backup Database
